Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

This policy explains what information Balance Buddy collects when you use this website and what we do with it. We keep it short because we keep it simple.

What we collect

If you fill in the “Reserve a seat” or “Book a free demo class” form, we collect the details you give us: your name, email address, phone number, your professional background, and any message you choose to add. We don't use tracking cookies or sell your data, and we don't collect anything you don't actively type into the form.

Why we collect it

Only to respond to you about the Balance Buddy cohort — to send the curriculum and start dates, answer your questions, and keep you updated about enrolment. That's it.

How it's stored

Form submissions are processed and stored by our hosting provider, Netlify (Netlify Forms), and a notification is emailed to us. We keep your details only as long as needed to follow up with you about the cohort.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing. We only use the service providers needed to run this site and contact you (e.g. Netlify for forms and our email provider).

Your choices

  • You can ask us to show you, correct, or delete the details you've submitted at any time.
  • You can opt out of further contact by replying to any message or emailing us.

Contact

Questions about your data, or want it removed? Email aditya@balancebuddy.com and we'll take care of it.
